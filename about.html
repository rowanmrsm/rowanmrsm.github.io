About, Docs and List of Sources

Hi, I'm Rowan, a fourth-year MMath student. This site is part of my Maths in Action project for my master's in mathematics at University of Warwick. It's been built with HTML to incorporate MathJax which makes the mathematical content possible to display. My aim in building this site was to explore different forms of science communication, complementing my scholarly report and magazine article. Limited space in all of the forms means there is a lot of content I was unable to cover, so I would especially reccomend Frank Gaitan's Quantum Error Correction and Fault Tolerant Quantum Computing, and Nielsen and Chuang's classic textbook Quantum Computing and Quantum Information. Thanks for checking it out!
